Dua & Supplication for Blessings

Prayer for the Developer, Family, and All of Us

🤲 Dua and Supplication for Blessings

Read with a calm heart, reflect on every word, and raise your hands with full hope to Allah...

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful...

O Allah, our Lord, we come to You with a sincere heart, bringing hope and deep supplication...

رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ

"Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and protect us from the punishment of the Fire." (Surah Al-Baqarah, verse 201)

O Allah, bestow blessings, guidance, goodness, safety, health, lawful and blessed sustenance, and happiness in this world and the hereafter upon the developer of this website, upon their parents, upon their family, upon their ancestors who have passed before them, upon us who are reading this dua, upon our families, and upon our ancestors as well.

O Allah, protect us all from all diseases, calamities, and dangers. Keep us away from the punishment of the Hellfire and admit us into Your Paradise filled with eternal blessings. Grant us guidance to always remain on the straight path of Islam.

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَلِوَالِدَيَّ وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ يَوْمَ يَقُومُ الْحِسَابُ

"My Lord, forgive me and my parents and the believers on the Day the account is established." (Surah Ibrahim, verse 41)

O Allah, forgive all our sins, the sins of our parents, and the sins of our ancestors. Make our families full of peace, love, and Your mercy. Accept all our good deeds and make easy all our affairs.

رَبَّنَا لَا تُزِغْ قُلُوبَنَا بَعْدَ إِذْ هَدَيْتَنَا وَهَبْ لَنَا مِن لَّدُنكَ رَحْمَةً ۚ إِنَّكَ أَنتَ الْوَهَّابُ

"Our Lord, let not our hearts deviate after You have guided us and grant us from Yourself mercy. Indeed, You are the Bestower." (Surah Ali Imran, verse 8)

Dear brother/sister... Every time this dua is read with sincerity, may Allah record it as a continuous good deed that flows without end. This dua becomes a bond of love between us, the developer, and all our families reaching back to our ancestors who have passed. May Allah gather us all in His Paradise. Aamiin.

🌙 Closing

O Allah, accept this dua from Your weak servants. Make every letter uttered become reward, every word become goodness, and every supplication become a means for us to connect with Your mercy.

May this dua unite our hearts, become a source of continuous goodness, and become a path for us to pray for one another with sincerity solely for Your sake.

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِلْمُسْلِمِينَ وَالْمُسْلِمَاتِ وَالْمُؤْمِنِينَ وَالْمُؤْمِنَاتِ الْأَحْيَاءِ مِنْهُمْ وَالْأَمْوَاتِ

"O Allah, forgive the Muslim men and women, the believing men and women, those who are living and those who have passed away."

آمِينَ يَا رَبَّ الْعَالَمِينَ

May Allah answer all our prayers... 🤲

Wassalamu'alaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh
May peace, mercy, and blessings of Allah subhanahu wa ta'ala be upon us all
